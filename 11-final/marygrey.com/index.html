About

Hello! I'm Mary.

Born in Saratoga Springs, NY.

A Graphics/Packaging Designer.

Living and loving Boston, MA.

I graduated from Ohio University in 2008 with a Bachelor of Science in Visual Communications of Informational Graphics/Publication Design and a specialization in Journalism. In addition to that, I also studied abroad in London, England for a summer with AIU (American International University) in Fashion and Journalism; best decision ever.

I'm a right brain kind of girl...I'm creative. I'm a free spirt. I'm passionate.

I'm enthusiastic, hard working and very well organized. I know how to mange my time wisely, while producing superior work on time and matching the clients needs. It's my desire to create something intriguing. I always inject all my energy into creating a successful end piece.

I'm a Buckeyes fan. I have lived in the Virgin Islands. I dislike hamburgers and peanut butter...I know, it's not normal.

I enjoy traveling, fashion, interior design and photography.

I look forward to the relationships I make.

Thanks!
